Our Policies

At Explore Travel, we strive to provide our customers with an exceptional travel experience. We understand that plans can change, and we offer flexible and transparent refund policies to ensure peace of mind when booking with us.

General Terms & Conditions

  • Refund Eligibility: Refund requests depend on booking type and timing, subject to terms outlined here.
  • Non-Refundable Fees: Certain fees such as administrative or processing fees are non-refundable once paid.

Cancellation Policy

  • More than 30 days before departure: Full refund excluding non-refundable fees.
  • 15 to 30 days before departure: 50% refund excluding non-refundable fees.
  • Less than 15 days before departure: No refund. Possible future travel credit subject to availability.

Special Circumstances for Refunds

  • Medical Emergencies: Cancellations due to medical emergencies will be reviewed case-by-case. Proof required.
  • Flight Cancellations or Delays: We will assist with rescheduling but are not responsible for airline issues.
  • Natural Disasters or Unforeseen Circumstances: Credits or partial refunds may be offered based on the situation.
